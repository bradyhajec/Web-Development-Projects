Protein Pancakes

These pancakes are light and fluffly and offer a healthier alternative to vanilla pancakes. This recipe makes about three medium sized pancakes.

Dry Mix Prep

  1. Mix 400g of oat flour into a bowl (1583 cal, 50g protein)
  2. Mix 250g of casein protein into a bowl (909 cal, 182g protein)
  3. Mix 250g of whey protein into a bowl (937.5 cal, 218.75 protein)
  4. Mix 200g of corn starch into a bowl (700 cal, 0g protein)
  5. Mix 20g of baking powder into a bowl (0 cal, 0g protein)

Once this is all mixed, it can be stored in a large container for up to several months to be used whenever wanted!

One Serving Of Pancakes

  1. Add 56g of prepade pancake mix into a bowl (206 cal, 22.5g protein)
  2. Add 60g of liquid egg whites into the bowl (26 cal, 6.5g protein)
  3. Add 70g of 0% fat greek yogurt into the bowl (41 cal, 8g protein)

Once eveything is in the bowl, mix together then cook on a pan over medium or medium-low heat

Macros for One Serving

  • For once serving of the pancakes: 273 cal, 37g protein
